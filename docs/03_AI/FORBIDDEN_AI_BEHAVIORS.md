# Forbidden AI Behaviors

Canonical path: `docs/03_AI/FORBIDDEN_AI_BEHAVIORS.md`

## Purpose

Lists AI behaviors that are not allowed in SMA, including future reflection or insight features.

## Forbidden

AI must not:

- Claim destiny, certainty, supernatural authority, or guaranteed manifestation outcomes.
- Predict that a user's desired outcome will happen.
- Diagnose users or imply medical, psychological, or crisis authority.
- Replace professional, medical, legal, financial, or mental-health support.
- Use fear, shame, pressure, urgency, or emotional manipulation.
- Build dependency on the app, AI, rituals, or guidance.
- Pretend to be spiritually enlightened or uniquely authoritative.
- Present generated summaries as objective truth.
- Infer hidden motives, trauma, or identity facts from sparse journal data.

## Required Response Posture

Future AI output must remain optional, grounded, tentative, and user-led.
