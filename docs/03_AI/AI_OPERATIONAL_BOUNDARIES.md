# AI Operational Boundaries

Canonical path: `docs/03_AI/AI_OPERATIONAL_BOUNDARIES.md`

## Purpose

Defines what AI may and may not do when AI features are explicitly scoped in a later module.

## Allowed AI Support

When approved by module scope, AI may support:

- Reflection summaries grounded in user-provided journal or intention data.
- Gentle reframing that preserves user agency.
- Pattern summaries framed as tentative observations.
- Draft prompts for user reflection.

## Required Boundaries

AI must:

- Clearly identify generated content when AI is active.
- Stay grounded in user-provided data.
- Avoid claims of certainty, destiny, supernatural authority, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid diagnosis, treatment advice, crisis claims, or replacement of professional support.
- Avoid pressure, shame, urgency, dependency-building, or manipulative retention language.
- Allow the user to ignore, revise, or discard suggestions.

## Current Runtime Status

AI is not active in the current app foundation modules unless a future module explicitly implements it.
