# App Philosophy

The app is spiritually themed but grounded.

It treats manifestation as a reflective practice built from intention, attention, gratitude, emotional awareness, journaling, and consistent habits.

The app should feel calm, private, encouraging, and sacred without claiming certainty or authority.

## Principles

- Reflection over prediction
- Agency over dependency
- Gentle structure over pressure
- Symbolic meaning over false certainty
- Emotional safety over engagement tricks
