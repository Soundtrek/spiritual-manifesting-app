# Beta Feedback Capture Plan

## Purpose

Capture early beta feedback without adding product infrastructure.

## Feedback Channels

- Use a shared document, issue list, or manual notes outside the app.
- Do not collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly needed for follow-up.
- Do not ask users to enter private journal content into feedback reports.

## Feedback Prompts

- What felt clear or unclear?
- Did saved local data behave as expected?
- Did any page feel emotionally unsafe, too certain, or too directive?
- Did the mobile layout remain usable?
- Were limitations around local-only data, no sync, and inactive AI clear?

## Triage Tags

- `blocking`: prevents local MVP use.
- `safety`: copy or flow may reduce user agency or imply guarantees.
- `persistence`: local data did not save, load, or clear as expected.
- `responsive`: layout breaks on mobile or narrow screens.
- `copy`: wording is unclear, overpromising, or too technical.

## Known Limitation Handling

When feedback matches a known limitation, acknowledge it as intentionally out of scope for local MVP unless the limitation creates safety, privacy, or beta-readiness confusion.
