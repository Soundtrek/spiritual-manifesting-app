# Ethical Boundaries

## Purpose

This app deals with personal hopes, emotions, beliefs, journaling, and future desires. That makes ethics central to the product.

## Ethical Position

The app supports reflection and intentional living. It does not sell certainty, miracles, supernatural proof, or emotional dependency.

## Boundaries

### No Exploitation

The app must not exploit vulnerability, loneliness, grief, financial stress, heartbreak, or fear.

### No False Authority

The app must not position itself as a spiritual master, therapist, doctor, psychic, prophet, or destiny reader.

### No Coercive Retention

The app must not use streaks, notifications, or AI language to shame users into returning.

### No Outcome Guarantees

The app must not imply that rituals, affirmations, journaling, or paid features guarantee results.

### No Hidden Manipulation

The app must not personalize content in ways that pressure users into purchases, dependency, or obsessive use.

## Product Standard

Every feature must answer:

- Does this preserve user agency?
- Is this emotionally safe?
- Is this honest about uncertainty?
- Is this useful without becoming manipulative?
