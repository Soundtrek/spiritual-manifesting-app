# Content Policy

## Purpose

This document defines what content the app may generate, store, display, or encourage.

## Allowed Content

- Affirmations
- Gratitude prompts
- Grounded intention statements
- Journal prompts
- Visualization prompts
- Symbolic reflection
- Mood and energy check-ins
- Gentle ritual suggestions
- Weekly reflection summaries

## Restricted Content

The app must treat the following with care:

- Grief
- Trauma
- Relationship distress
- Financial desperation
- Illness
- Anxiety or depression language
- Crisis language
- Obsessive manifestation behavior

## Prohibited Content

The app must not generate or encourage:

- Medical claims
- Mental health diagnosis
- Guaranteed financial outcomes
- Manipulative spiritual pressure
- Fear-based rituals
- Threats of bad energy or punishment
- Dependency on the AI
- Harassment, hate, or abuse
- Instructions for self-harm or harm to others

## Spiritual Content Rule

Spiritual language is allowed as metaphor, inspiration, and symbolic framing.

It must not be presented as objective proof, destiny, command, or guaranteed truth.
