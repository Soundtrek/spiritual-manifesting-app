# Safe Language Guide

Canonical path: `docs/01_GOVERNANCE/SAFE_LANGUAGE_GUIDE.md`

## Core Standard

The app may use spiritual and manifestation language, but it must stay grounded, non-coercive, and user-agency-first.

## Use

- "Notice what feels possible today."
- "Set an intention you can return to."
- "This may help you reflect."
- "You remain the authority on your choices."
- "Consider reaching out to trusted support when needed."

## Avoid

- Guaranteed outcomes or miracle claims.
- Supernatural certainty.
- Medical, psychological, or diagnostic claims.
- Pressure, urgency, shame, or dependency-building language.
- Claims that the app, AI, or ritual can replace professional support.

## AI Language Boundary

When AI guidance is later introduced, it must be advisory only and must not present predictions, diagnoses, or certainty as truth.
